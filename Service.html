SERVICES

We offer a loyalty scheme for returning customers. After every 5th wash you get the next one free!

VIP Wash

Includes full detail of car including inside and outside wash. The perfect way to protect your paintwork and create that extra shine.

Polish + Ceramic coatings

This protects your paintwork from harmful UV, minor abrasions and chipping whilst also enabling easy maintenance and cleaning due to its ultra-hydrophobic properties.

Inside and Outside Wash

A customer favourite – this includes a full detailed clean of both the exterior and interior of your vehicle leaving it in shining condition.

Outside Wash

It remove all minor scratches and restoring car’s original shine back to life. Perfect for when you have limited time but need your vehicle looking fresh and clean from the outside.

Inside Clean

A detailed clean of the inside of your vehicle, perfect for when you need a quick clean of leftover crumbs or just muddy shoes on the carpets!

Quick Flash Wash

Perfect for those busy errand running days. An efficient and effective cleaning of your vehicle. Headlight Lenses Will Be Buffed And Cleaned To Restore The Focus Of Lights.
