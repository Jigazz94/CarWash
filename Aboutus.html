About Flash Car

Here at the flash car wash our number one priority is customer satisfaction. We are passionate and driven by customer service and top-class service, we strive to provide exceptional results at an attainable price. Our use of the latest technology and the industry’s best materials, you can rest assured that we will give your vehicle the best care possible.

Facilities

We have an in-house personal car wash with the best materials and equipment to ensure high-quality results every time. Our workshop is fitted with top-of-the-range LED so that our staff are able to work in the winter when it gets dark earlier so there is no room for error.

Our Staff

Our staff are not only friendly but also extremely knowledgeable in their field. We hire the best of the best so customers are satisfied knowing the people taking care of their vehicle have not only knowledge but also experience. Our staff are able to go through each service and aid in the process of deciding which service is better suited for you and your car.

Dream Car Spa is a service oriented Car Spa & Car Detailing company, established in the year 2012 in the heart of the city London, UK. We are specialised in Car Coating and expert in Car Cleaning of vehicles belonging to all categories.
