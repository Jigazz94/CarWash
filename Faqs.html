FAQs

We're here to help

How can I make a booking?

We have included a booking form on each page, fill out the form to book your slot. Alternatively, please call us on 07397553729!

Do you do walk-in bookings?

Although there is no promise there will be availability, on some occasions, we are free to do walk-in booking however please do call us beforehand to avoid disappointment.

Do I need to empty my car before hand?

To ensure we complete the service within the time frame, please remove all personal belongings from your vehicle.

How do I cancel a booking I’ve made?

You can contact us to cancel a booking, there is no fee for cancelling as long as we are given a minimum of 5 hours notice.

How do I redeem my free car wash from the loyalty scheme?

You can contact us to cancel a booking, there is no fee for cancelling as long as we are given a minimum of 5 hours’ notice.
